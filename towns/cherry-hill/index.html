Why Cherry Hill Is a High-Value Market

  • High homeowner density
  • Strong 15–30 year exterior replacement cycles
  • Storm exposure (wind, hail, seasonal weather)
  • Large residential neighborhoods ideal for canvassing

Primary Vertical: Roofing

Cherry Hill homes include colonials, ranch properties, and split-level homes with aging asphalt systems. NJLeads identifies:

  • Wind-damaged shingles
  • Granule loss
  • Curling and lifted tabs
  • Hail impact indicators
  • Visible aging and discoloration

Siding Leads

We detect siding wear, storm damage, and aging materials including:

  • Cracked or warped vinyl siding
  • Faded or chalking exterior panels
  • Loose or missing sections
  • Storm-related impact damage

Window Replacement Opportunities

NJLeads flags visible window aging indicators such as:

  • Older single-pane or dated frame styles
  • Seal failure signs
  • Warped or deteriorating trim
  • Energy-inefficient installations

Driveway Replacement Leads

Driveways are high-visibility and often overlooked lead sources. We detect:

  • Cracking and surface deterioration
  • Heaving or settling slabs
  • Severe asphalt breakdown
  • Visible patchwork repairs

Fence Replacement Opportunities

Fences frequently show visible aging. We identify:

  • Leaning or damaged panels
  • Storm impact damage
  • Rotting wood fencing
  • Outdated or deteriorated materials
